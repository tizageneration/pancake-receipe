pancake

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 3/4 cup milk
  • cups self-raising flour
  • butter for frying, plus extra to serve
  • l/3 cup caster sugar
  • butter for frying plus extra for to serve
  • maple syrup to serve

Method

  • whisk eggs, milk and vanilla together in a jug.
  • flour into a large bowl and stir sugar in it
  • add all mixtures and mix until well combined
  • heat your non-stick frying pan over medium heat
  • cook for 2 minutes until bubbles appear on the surface
  • and cook for another 2 minutes
  • with maple syrup and extra butter
